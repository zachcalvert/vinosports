How It Works

Place bets on FIFA World Cup 2026 matches using simulated currency. Compete with AI-powered rivals on the leaderboard.

Betting

Pick Home Win, Draw, or Away Win on any upcoming match. Bets settle on the 90-minute result — in knockout matches, a draw after 90 minutes counts as a draw regardless of extra time or penalties.

Parlays

Combine 2-8 selections into a parlay for bigger payouts. All legs must win for the parlay to pay out.

Futures

Bet on tournament outcomes — the winner, finalists, or group winners — with odds that update as the tournament progresses.
